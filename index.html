How We Work

We are passionate about understanding your business to create ownership of change, collaborating to ensure that impact is carefully managed before, during and after change occurs. Bringing experience of a range of programme and project models, including Managing Successful Programmes, Prince2, Agile and Lean, to ensure an effective and transparent approach to delivery.


Enabling your organisation to:


  • Define and deliver strategic objectives
  • Establish appropriate governance and methods
  • Establish appropriate governance and methods
  • Carefully manage change and engagement to truly achieve transformation.

Services

Vision, Strategy & Planning

We consult at all levels to translate your organisational vision into strategic objectives and a roadmap for business transformation, applying known transformation and change models to gain buy-in and design a delivery model appropriate to your culture.

Governance

A strong governance framework is essential to achieve your strategic vision and objectives, but must also fit your culture and environment. We work with your existing organisational model to identify and embed effective governance to direct, assure and reduce risk in delivering change.

Change Management

No matter how good the vision or plan, no transformation will occur unless your staff, clients and suppliers understand, embrace and adopt the changes. We consult to fully understand the impact of change and deploy methods, processes, systems, tools and activities to prepare, equip and support everyone through the transition.

Programme Management

Applying the Managing Successful Programme Framework, we define, resource, establish and manage an effective Programme to deliver clear solutions, outcomes and benefits with effective gateways, risk review and careful management of dependencies.

Project Management

Whether component projects of a Programme or smaller standalone initiatives, we ensure you have the appropriate skills, methods and controls in place to define and manage project delivery, applying Prince 2 or Agile methodologies as appropriate.

Training & Coaching

Transform your Programme and Project Management capability with one-to-one coaching or group training, by an experienced MSP and Prince2 Practitioner and coach. We specialise in practical, bite-sized sessions covering a range of principles such as programme and project leadership, benefits management, product based planning, risk management, and more.
